About the Founder

‼️ Who is Ayegbusi Olabanji?

Ayegbusi Olabanji is the Founder and CEO of Dynamic Athletics Media and Sports Management Ltd. He is a certified and highly experienced athletics coach, sports manager, and media professional with extensive expertise in the athletics ecosystem. He is also a certified Basic Life Saver (BLS) and an accomplished event and meeting organiser.

Through Dynamic Athletics Media and Sports Management Ltd, he consistently delivers well structured athletics competitions that create meaningful opportunities for both emerging and elite athletes across Nigeria.

Olabanji served as the Media Officer for Team Nigeria at the CAA Region II Senior Athletics Championships in Togo in 2023 and played a pivotal role in managing Team Nigeria athletics social media presence. He is a current member of the Athletics Federation of Nigeria (AFN) Media Committee.

His media platform, Dynamic Athletics Hub, stands out as one of Nigerias leading athletics focused outlets, widely respected for its comprehensive coverage of both local and international events. He has reported on major championships including the African Games, African Championships, National Championships, and the African Athletics Region II Championships.

In 2025, Olabanji partnered with the Athletics Federation of Nigeria to host one of the most talked about meets of the year, the AFN/Dynamic Athletics Meet in Lagos, further cementing his influence within Nigerias athletics community.
